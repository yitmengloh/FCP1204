Doom The Dark Ages Coming Soon!

DOOM: The Dark Ages is a ferocious new chapter in id Software’s iconic franchise — a prequel and reimagining that thrusts the DOOM Slayer into a time of ancient evil, medieval warfare, and unholy power. Set in a twisted dark fantasy world long before the events of modern DOOM, this entry blends brutal first-person combat with the gothic aesthetic of medieval Europe.

Players wield brutal melee weapons like the Slayer’s spiked mace and serrated greatsword, alongside archaic ballistic weaponry powered by hell-forged energy. The iconic double-barrel shotgun returns — now redesigned as a runed relic passed down by a forgotten order. Magic also plays a role, as players channel corrupted runes and relics to cast devastating hellfire and summon spectral shields.

Environments range from haunted cathedrals and rotting kingdoms to hellish citadels where demons reign supreme. Each level is hand-crafted for high-speed traversal and cinematic combat flow, with vertical combat zones, destructible structures, and enemy ambush events. The game is powered by a next-gen engine, delivering hyper-detailed gore, dynamic lighting, and massive set-piece boss battles that echo both myth and madness.

The soundtrack, composed by a fusion of Gregorian chants and heavy metal, intensifies the grim tone of the game. In The Dark Ages, the Slayer is not just a destroyer — he's a cursed warrior, hunted by prophecy, and destined to either save or doom a crumbling world. Prepare for the most savage, stylistic, and story-driven DOOM experience yet.
