Valorant is a free-to-play tactical first-person shooter (FPS) developed and published by Riot Games. Released in 2020, the game features 5v5 team-based gameplay, where players control agents with unique abilities. It combines precise gunplay with strategic ability usage, similar to a mix between CS:GO and Overwatch. The game emphasizes teamwork, skill, and quick decision-making.

Genre: First-Person Shooter (FPS), Tactical Shooter, Competitive Multiplayer
