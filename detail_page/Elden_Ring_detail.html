Elden Ring is an open-world action RPG developed by FromSoftware and published by Bandai Namco in 2022. Created by Hidetaka Miyazaki with world-building contributions from George R.R. Martin, the game offers a vast fantasy world, deep lore, and challenging combat. It features a non-linear open world called The Lands Between, allowing players to explore dungeons, defeat powerful bosses, and customize their playstyle with different weapons and magic. The game is praised for its difficulty, rich world-building, and rewarding gameplay.

Genre: Action RPG, Open World, Fantasy

Device: PC, PlayStation, Xbox
